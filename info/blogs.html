You've reached an index to my blogs

It was in 2012 that I started writing. I started writing about cricket which at one point was very close to my heart. But, has slowly faded.

My love for writing has stayed with me, however and this is one of the reasons why I decided to add this blog to my website.

I've brought to life a personal, a technical and a random thoughts blog. I haven't killed the cricket blog, yet and hope to give it a restart, maybe a few years down the lane.

I am a staunch believer that keeping things recorded helps one make better decisions in the future, whether it is regarding personal or professional life. Hence, I've started writing in these blogs. I could have chosen a more social network integrated approach to creation of these blogs. But, I did not want to do that because these blogs are representative of my thoughts at the time of writing every post and I do not want those thoughts to reach the mass at a lightning fast rate which social networks can easily do. I believe in percolation of thoughts in these blogs through the several layers of a network of people. Hope you enjoy what you read here. Do leave comments if you have anything to say about a post irrespective of whether it is positive or negative.
