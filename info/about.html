Professional Background

  1. College

    Went to college at PES Institute of Technology, Bangalore, India. Graduated in 2011 with a Bachelor's degree in Information Science and Engineering.

  2. Work

    Joined Ittiam Systems Pvt. Ltd. in July 2011, now a company focussed on digital media solutions, back then had a communications solution division. Worked on a lot of interesting projects on MAC layer in WLANs. Improving reliability of multicast traffic in WLANs, designing and implementing standard IEEE 802.11 MAC proctocols and custom protocols to name a few.

    Ensuring Wi-Fi certification and Chip tapeouts were a success for many customers worldwide was an interesting phase in my life. It required me to travel to Shanghai at several times, helped me gain a better understanding of how people in general and to be specific, Software developers go about their day-to-day activities in the workplace.

    Later on, the focus in the company moved more towards the digital media solutions and as part of this, I started with developing multimedia protocols like Closed Captioning and later moved on to designing a web application for transcoding for video on demand as well as live stream. Although this was for a short period, the experience in participating in such a design motivated me to move towards working in the application domain.

  3. Grad School

    I started Grad School at Arizona State University in January 2015 and graduated in July 2016. Pursuing a Master's degree in Compter Science, I've taken courses that are more relevant to the software development in the user space. I've focussed my efforts in learning more in the areas of application development, data sciences and database systems.

    I also took up a part-time job with a PhD. student where I was required to design and develop a desktop application for generation and visualization of datasets using Java and interfacing with excel. He used this application as a tool for demonstartion of his work during his thesis defense.

  4. Work Again

    Joined Brightcove in September 2016 which is a media focussed company with main intensions of video delivery either on demand or through live mediums. I work on the back-end of the video system, pretty much covering different video protocols to handling storage of video or user data on either file systems or databases, handling queueing mechanisms, notifications and more such common complexities of a video system on a cloud platform.
