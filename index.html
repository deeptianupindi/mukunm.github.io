Interests

I've been interested in the core concepts of Computer Science and I'm always looking to understand something in technology if there's something new and I don't know about it.

  1. Web applications

    I've always been interested in developing web applications even while I've been involved in other development activities. I belive that however strong the tech at the back is, the application also plays an equal part in getting consumers to use and understand the tech behind it and that's what interests me. Although, I haven't developed many large scale applications, I do understand the concepts behind them. I am briefly familiar with Spring, Yii, Django and ASP .Net. I do intend to work on a few web applications in the future.

  2. Machine Learning

    In the past year and a half, I've learnt a lot about machine learning through courses at ASU. I've dealt with python libraries SciPy when I've developed projects for these courses. From all of this work, I've come to understand that all of machine learning problems are optimization problems and we're trying to find a minimum or maximum for the optimization function. I've learnt about classification algorithms mainly. I feel that I've a lot of things to understand in this field and intend to learn more in the upcoming year.

  3. Multimedia

    Over time, at different phases, I've worked on certain multimedia protocols. I've worked on Closed Captioning protocols in the industry and carried out course projects for frame matching using feature extraction through DCT and DWT for different block sizes. I am quite interested in this field, although my knowledge in this field is limited and would like to see how my knowledge in this comes to use in the future because multimedia is going to exist forever given how much social media we have nowadays.
